# Contributing to Prism

This public repository contains proprietary software and brand materials
owned by Tradestreaks LLC. It is not an open-source project, and public access
does not grant permission to reuse its contents.

Tradestreaks LLC does not accept unsolicited contributions. Authorized
contributors must have a written agreement covering confidentiality and
assignment of applicable intellectual-property rights before submitting work.

Do not submit code, designs, copy, data, or other material that:

- belongs to a current or former employer, client, or other third party;
- is governed by a license incompatible with Prism's proprietary use;
- contains confidential information that Tradestreaks LLC is not authorized
  to receive; or
- was copied from another product without permission.

## Pull-request declaration

By opening an authorized pull request, the contributor confirms that:

- the contribution is original or properly authorized and licensed;
- all third-party material and its license have been disclosed;
- the contribution does not knowingly infringe another party's rights; and
- any required contributor agreement is already on file.

AI-assisted work must be reviewed by a human contributor. The pull request
should identify material AI assistance when it produced an appreciable amount
of code, copy, or artwork, and the contributor remains responsible for
checking the result for copied material and incompatible dependencies.

Preserve copyright, attribution, and license notices that accompany
third-party materials. Questions should be sent to
support@tradestreaks.com.
